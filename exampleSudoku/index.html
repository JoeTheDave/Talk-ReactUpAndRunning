Click on any square to select it, move around with the arrorw keys.
Hit escape to deselect the active square.
Use keyboard numbers to enter a value into the active square.
Hold down shift while entering a number to enter a clue instead.
Space bar will erase a previously entered number.
Hold shift while hitting space bar will erase all clue marks in the select square.
Hold ctrl while hitting space bar will erase all clue marks in the entire grid.
Hold down shift while hitting '?' key to enter active clues mode. (red marks)
Hold down shift while hitting '?' key again to exit active clues mode. (blue marks)
Hold ctrl and hit '?' key to show correct answers.
Hold ctrl and hit '?' key again to hide correct answers.
